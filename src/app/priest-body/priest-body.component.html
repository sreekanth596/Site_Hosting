Priest Lalitha Krishna Sharma has dedicated more than 25 years of his life to serving as a Hindu Religious priest. Throughout his career, he has successfully performed countless pujas, homam's, and various religious ceremonies for people from diverse backgrounds across the globe. His extensive expertise and profound knowledge make him an invaluable asset to the Hindu Society.

Lalitha Krishna Sharma ji is a highly esteemed and renowned priest in the RTP Area, which includes Raleigh, Cary, Apex, Holly Springs, Fuquay-Varina, Garner, Greensboro, Charlotte, and more. With an extensive track record, he has successfully conducted over 500 Gruhapravesams in the RTP Area, showcasing his vast experience in this field. Additionally, he has officiated more than 300 marriages and performed various other significant rituals such as Ganapati Homam, Satyanaraya Swamy Vratam, Chandi Homam, Rudra Abhishekam, Sudarsana Homam, Sri Rama Kalyanam, Shiva Kalyanam, Navagraha Japan, Nakshatra shanti, and many more.

Lalitha Krishna Sharma

Education

Sri Lalitha Krishna Sharma ji developed a strong interest in Vedic teachings at a young age and enrolled in the prestigious Shri Sudharma Rakshana Parishad, an esteemed institution in Andhra Pradesh, to pursue Vedic learning. Under the guidance of Bramhasri Piratla Ramavadhani Garu and Bramhasri Vishnubhotla Anjaneya Sharma Garu, he received a comprehensive education in subjects like Krishna Yajurvedam, Panchadasa Karma, and Astrology. His dedication and expertise in Krishna Yajurveda earned him the title of "Vedabramha" from Sringeri Shankaracharya Maha Samsthanam in Karnataka. Additionally, he furthered his knowledge by studying Yajurveda Poorva and Apara Prayogam. Upon relocating to Hyderabad in 2001, he continued his education in Astrology and Vastu under the guidance of his father and various other Gurus.
